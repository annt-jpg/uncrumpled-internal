Design notes

  • Slide 1 hook = BOLDER TYPE. Heaviest weight on whole carousel. Bigger size than phase headlines. Stops the scroll. Test at thumbnail size — must read on grid.
  • Text-led — no photos required. Canva template friendly.
  • Phase headlines styled BIG. 3-word descriptor below. Bullets smaller. Undercut italic at bottom.
  • Slide 5 = standalone naming reveal. Big type pop. Consider black background + white text.
  • Slide 6 = launch flag — "Introducing the new Uncrumpled" headline BIG, "coming soon" italic tease at bottom.
  • Type C explainer + contrast pairs do NOT live on slides — moved to caption (long-read for curious scrollers).

Why this works

  • Slide-light read. 1 idea per slide. Audience flicks fast → stops at slide 5 reveal.
  • Caption holds the depth. Curious read more, casual still get the gist.
  • Founder transparency at a pivot = high trust signal.
  • Slide 6 = launch flag with "coming soon" tease — builds anticipation for what's next.
  • Fills 📣 Brand POV slot in surface mix (was 1, now 2).
