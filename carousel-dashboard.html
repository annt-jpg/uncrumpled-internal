Pre-post Production Checklist (every carousel)

Hook slide tested at thumbnail size — readable on grid view
Max 2 lines text per slide — italics for the "reality undercut"
Slide 1 = hook + content #1 baked together (saves a slide)
Last slide = sign-off + bio nudge (rotate the bank, no repeat in same week)
Brand colours + lockup consistent (Canva master template)
Caption ≤ 2 lines + 1 CTA + #CertifiedChaos hashtag
Banned-word scrub: no "perfect/perfection/scientifically/iron" as our product
"Spray" not "steam" or "iron" when mentioning Uncrumpled
Inclusive framing — Type C inclusive of any persona that relates
Permission captured if reposting any community content
