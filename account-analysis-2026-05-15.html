Pattern analysis

What's working · what's untapped

Across 50 ads, 5 formats and 6 angle archetypes. Below: what scales, what to leave alone, what we haven't tested at all.

Static problem-hooks lead on efficiency

FORMAT · SCALE UP

Big-text static "S" series (S1, S2, S3) dominate the top of the ROAS table. S3 Creases least fav hit 5.34x. S3 Not the only one who hates ironing carries $2,528 spend at 3.08x.

  • Why it works: typography-led, on-feed legibility, identity-call ("you hate ironing too, right?")
  • What to do: spin 4-6 more S statics per quarter. Cheapest format to produce, highest ROAS bracket.

V4 Real-time demos = volume workhorse

FORMAT · KEEP RUNNING

V4 Real time (Checkered shirt, Drawer to bed) and V2 In real time (Green shirt) sit at positions 2, 4, 8, 10, 14, 18 — that's 6 of the top 20. Total V series spend ~$2,200 in 30d.

  • Why it works: proof. Watch the wrinkle drop in real time, no edits, no doubt.
  • What to do: shoot 2-3 more real-time clips per month. Different garments (linen, silk, denim) to widen audience.

"Big text identity call" pattern is underbuilt

ANGLE · SCALE UP NOW

S6 "For that piece you love" + S3 "Not the only one who hates ironing" both lean on the same pattern: big serif typography calling out a specific identity moment. 2.24x ROAS and 2.02% CTR on the blue shorts variant — well above account CTR average.

  • What's hitting: "For that [garment] you love" · "We both know you won't iron" · "Not the only one who hates ironing"
  • What to do: 6-8 new variants on the same identity-call pattern (see test ideas below)

Founder cam = highest CTR in the account

FORMAT · UNDER-INVESTED

V1 Hi I am Ann pulled 4.14% CTR — almost 2x account average. V7 Ann black dress and other Ann-to-camera variants all sit in the 1.91-2.87% CTR bracket. But total founder spend is only ~$485 / 4.5% of account spend.

  • Why under-invested: production friction (Ann on camera) + memory note: she explicitly doesn't want daily founder pressure
  • What to do: 1 founder shoot per month, cut into 3-4 ad variants. Low effort, high signal.

Travel angle: 0 dedicated ads, huge product fit

ANGLE · GREENFIELD

Search across all 50 ads for travel / carry-on / suitcase / cabin / packing returns nothing. Only "blue shorts" is travel-vibe-adjacent. The 100ml travel bottle launches in June 2026. Audience demand is signalled in emails ("a lot of you have been asking" — per product launch email).

  • Why now: travel bottle launch = new SKU + new use-case occasion to claim
  • What to do: 4-5 travel-specific ads (carry-on demo, hotel room, packed-bag flatlay, wedding-guest weekend, festival packing). See ideas section.

TT-reply format = dead at the moment

FORMAT · KEEP BURNED

V2 TT reply Linen shirt = $0 spend last 30d. Per memory 2026-05-05: format looks dated even though it once converted. Ann's call already locked.

  • What to do: don't relaunch. Reuse the underlying linen visual in a different frame.
