Production rules

Format locks

Aspect ratios
1:1 (1080x1080) + 4:5 (1080x1350) + 9:16 (1080x1920) per ad
Palette
Cream #F5EFE2 · Sage deep #5C6E50 · Ink #2C2A24
Typography
Fraunces serif lowercase wordmark · sans for body callouts
Lockup
"uncrumpled" wordmark top-left every mock. Sticker chip rotated -8deg for discount badge.
CTA
Lead gen = "Sign Up" · Sale live = "Shop Now"
Land page
Lead gen → presale signup form/LP · Sale live → product/category page with 25% applied
Campaign
LG = Sales objective with Lead conversion event OR Leads objective + IG form · SL = Sales objective + Add to Cart/Purchase event
Audience
LG = cold + warm (broad AU 25-65 women) · SL = retargeting LG signups + cold + warm

Locks needed before launch

  • Confirm 25% off (vs 30% in existing EOFY image-ad pack)
  • Lock sale start + end dates
  • Lock LP for lead gen (signup form built + thank-you page)
  • Confirm Was/Now price for SL-03 (need RRP × 0.75)
  • Travel bottle + refill pack final assets shot
  • Naming convention applied per Uncrumpled Meta naming structure
  • Meta pixel events firing: Lead + Purchase
  • Lead gen audience suppressed from sale-live retargeting (already converted)

Why this split works

  • → Lead gen 7-14 days pre-sale = builds warm list before discount reveal. Klaviyo + Meta retargeting compound.
  • → "Don't buy yet" pattern interrupt in LG-04 stops scroll, mirrors Email 1 of EOFY sequence.
  • → "Two sales a year" + "Next sale not till November" anchors are evergreen scarcity, not invented urgency.
  • → 4 angle variants in sale live (discount / scarcity / sign / new product / urgency) = test creative against same offer.
  • → New-product call-outs (SL-05, SL-08, SL-09) carry the launch hype into the sale window.
  • → SL-10 "48 hours left" runs final 2 days only, same anchor as email 5.

Burned / don't do

  • "Don't miss out" / "Hurry" / "Today only" hard sell
  • Type C / Certified Chaos identity copy in sale lane (kept DR per EOFY pack rule)
  • Em dashes (Ann scrub rule)
  • Compound-word hyphens unless natural (carry-on OK, "best-deal" not)
  • Top-right corner pill chips (RO designer feedback — RO reads as production asset)
  • Split-screen Type B vs Type C (killed in 2026-05-01 lock)
  • Fake countdown timer GIFs
  • "Once in a lifetime" / "biggest sale ever" if not literally true
  • Pricing claims without confirmed RRP (don't lock $33 → $24.75 until verified)
  • Same headline reused across multiple ads (Meta dedupe)
